My Philosophy

The thinking behind the work

01

Define your own success metrics. Society's broad standards are reference, not mandate. I guide myself toward work that feels genuinely meaningful rather than chasing externally defined success.

02

Cyberpunk sparked the obsession. My pursuit of AI traces back to Ghost in the Shell — the boundary between human and machine, the nature of consciousness. This isn't just a career; it's a worldview.

03

Technology serves business reality. Companies need monetization. The best model finds the exact balance between technical precision and business output — not paper-optimal solutions.

04

First principles. Facing a complex problem, strip all assumptions and derive from the most fundamental logic — not convention or empiricism.

05

Kill the curse of knowledge. Expressing complex ideas in the simplest possible language so others understand in 30 seconds — this is a severely underrated core skill.

06

Underdog mindset, stay humble. Earlier in life I just wanted to play games — no fancy background. Finding ML/AI gave me real intrinsic drive. That journey keeps me from getting complacent, and from underestimating anyone.

07

Go to the bottom of the code and the logic. Not satisfied with calling APIs and skimming docs — I want to know why it works the way it does, and what happens when you change the approach.
